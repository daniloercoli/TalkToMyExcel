Why it matters

An Excel spreadsheet can be more sensitive than a document.

Business spreadsheets often contain price lists, margins, tickets, defects, serial numbers, customer records and operational notes. Choosing any provider just for convenience can expose the company to risks that are hard to explain later.

With Regolo.ai, TalkToMyExcel can be presented as an AI experience oriented around data sovereignty: European, controllable, compatible with standard endpoints and without retention on the recommended provider side.
