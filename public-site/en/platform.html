Platform

From spreadsheet to answer, without turning everything into indistinct text.

TalkToMyExcel treats tabular data as tabular data: rows, columns, filters, counts and descriptive text remain separate and controllable elements.

01

Upload the file

Excel, CSV, TSV and Parquet files are saved in staging and profiled before replacing or adding to the active dataset.

02

Choose what to index

The system suggests the descriptive columns most useful for semantic search, but the final choice remains explicit.

03

Ask the data

Questions about counts and status go through DuckDB; similar cases and descriptions go through Chroma.

04

Read a cited answer

The model generates the answer from compact evidence, not from the entire file pasted into a prompt.

Formats and scenarios

One entry point for exports, reports and spreadsheets.

Current inputs

Excel workbooks `.xlsx/.xls`, `.csv`, `.tsv` and `.parquet` files. They are the simplest starting point for business datasets you already have.

Business use

After-sales, maintenance, tickets, quality, price lists, spare parts, CRM exports and periodic reports: different data, same question experience.

Hybrid engine

Precise numbers when numbers matter. Semantics when meaning matters.

An LLM alone can be brilliant but approximate. TalkToMyExcel uses it as a reasoning and synthesis layer, while filters, rows and counts stay handled by dedicated data components.
