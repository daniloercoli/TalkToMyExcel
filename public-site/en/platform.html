Platform

From spreadsheet to decision, with a conversation that follows the work.

TalkToMyExcel preserves data structure, selects the right analysis and lets you explore results through natural follow-up questions.

How TalkToMyExcel works

01

Upload one or more files

Excel, CSV, TSV and Parquet files are checked before entering the user's dedicated workspace.

02

Confirm the useful data

Choose the sheets, tables and descriptive fields to include. The system suggests a selection; the decision stays explicit.

03

Ask and follow up

Start with one question and continue with related requests: recent context helps keep the thread.

04

Verify the answer

The result is grounded in selected evidence and shows tables or source rows when the analysis path makes them available.
