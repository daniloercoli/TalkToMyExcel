Not a generic chatbot

When a spreadsheet contains customers, margins, tickets or operations data, security becomes part of the product.

TalkToMyExcel is built for companies that want to use AI on tabular business data without losing traceability. The system separates file handling, dataset import, the data engine and answer generation.

Regolo.ai is the recommended provider when European sovereignty, zero retention and payload control are concrete requirements, not footnotes.

What it does

From file to decision, without losing the thread.

Upload one or more tabular files and ask questions in natural language. TalkToMyExcel chooses the right method and keeps the context needed for your next question.

Multi-file workspace

Excel, CSV, TSV and Parquet files can share one analysis workspace, kept separate for each user.

A conversation that keeps context

After the first answer, ask “only the open ones” or “compare them by priority” without starting over.

Analysis matched to the question

Counts, filters, similar cases, comparisons and calculations use specialized paths that can work together.

Verifiable results

Results and tables stay visible; source rows are shown when the analysis path produces them. Even an empty result is reported without forcing an answer.

Business datasets

Start with the files your company already uses.

Reports, ERP exports, after-sales data and operational spreadsheets come together in one workspace. Add or remove datasets over time and keep querying them through a consistent experience.
